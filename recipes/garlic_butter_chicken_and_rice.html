Garlic Butter Chicken and Rice


Description

In this one-pan garlic butter chicken and rice recipe, chicken thighs and rice cook together in a rich, garlicky butter sauce. With minimal prep and just one pot to clean, it is the perfect meal for busy weeknights when you want big flavor without the fuss.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs (about 6)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, or more as needed, divided
  • 8 cloves garlic, minced, divided
  • 1/4 cup butter, cut into pieces
  • 1 1/2 cups long-grain white rice, rinsed
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, for garnish
  • 2 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese, for garnish

Steps

  1. Sprinkle chicken on both sides with salt, onion powder, black pepper, and paprika. Raw chicken pieces placed on a cutting board with seasoning being sprinkled by a hand
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a 5- to 6-quart Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the chicken thighs. Cook until seared and lightly browned, 4 to 5 minutes on each side. Transfer to a plate. Chicken thighs cooking in a large pan on a stovetop seasoned and lightly browned
  3. Reduce heat to medium. If needed, add remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil to the Dutch oven. Stir in 6 cloves minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Preparation of garlic and butter in a pan being stirred with a spatula
  4. Add 1/4 cup butter. When melted, stir in rice until fully coated. Pour in chicken broth and scrape the bottom of the Dutch oven with a wooden spoon to loosen any browned bits. Bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes. A hand stirring a mixture in a pot on a stovetop
  5. Combine melted butter and remaining 2 cloves minced garlic in a small bowl; set aside. Hand holding a spoon stirring garlic butter mixture in a glass bowl
  6. Nestle chicken thighs into the rice mixture. Brush tops of chicken with garlic butter mixture. Chicken being browned in a pan with garlic butter sauce hand basting the chicken with the sauce
  7. Cover and continue cooking until the rice has absorbed all the liquid and the chicken is cooked through (an instant-read thermometer inserted into the thickest part should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)), 10 to 15 minutes more. A dish of garlic butter chicken thighs and rice in a blue pot
  8. Sprinkle with parsley and Parmesan before serving. A plate with garlic butter chicken served over rice garnished with herbs and a fork on the side
