Chicken Arroz Caldo


Description

Arroz caldo is a comforting and flavorsome Filipino rice and chicken soup served mainly as a snack but can be enjoyed any time of the day.



Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (2 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 ¼ pounds chicken wings, split and tips discarded
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce (Optional)
  • 5 ¼ cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup glutinous sweet rice
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 lemon, sliced (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce for sprinkling, if desired (Optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Cook and stir onion, garlic, and ginger in hot oil until fragrant, about 5 minutes. A large pot with chopped onion, garlic, and ginger being stirred with a wooden spoon
  2. Add chicken wings; cook and stir for 1 minute. Stir in fish sauce, cover, and cook another 2 minutes. A large pot with chicken wings, fish sauce, chopped onion, garlic, and ginger
  3. Pour chicken broth into the pot. Stir in sweet rice and bring mixture to a boil. Cover and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent rice from sticking to the bottom of the pot. A large pot with chicken broth and rice added to the chicken wing-onion mixture
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Portion soup into bowls and garnish with green onion. A pot of chicken arroz caldo (chicken rice porridge), with two portion in bowls, served with slice green onions and lemon slices on the side
  5. Serve with lemon slices and additional fish sauce, if desired. A bowl of chicken arroz caldo (chicken rice porridge) garnished with sliced green onions and sliced lemon
